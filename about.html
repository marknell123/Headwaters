The HCI Philosophy and Goals

﻿​

As the name entails, Headwaters College, Inc. aims to be not only a source of quality and total (intellectual, physical, emotional and social) learning,

but of Christian development as well.It is HCI’s vision to be able to see our future graduates as God-fearing, people-loving, loyal citizens and outstanding individuals.

HCI calls it our “3 Ps” - Piety, Patriotism and Prestige. To nurture “children towards loving God, fellow countrymen and self” is therefore our mission by providing

them a better and conducive “haven” for study, exceptional teachers, advanced curricula, state-of-the-art instructional materials and God-centered institution.

It is HCI task to journey with our pupils and students in their learning and development towards achieving their lives’ ambitions and goals… marked with HCI’s 3Ps.
